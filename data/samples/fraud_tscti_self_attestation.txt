ANALYSIS NOTES (for fraud-check tool):
This email asks the candidate to self-report the four fields that a credentialed FSO
already receives directly from DISS JVS upon running a Joint Verification System query.
A legitimate FSO querying DISS JVS gets: Eligibility Level, Eligibility Determination,
CE Date, and Investigation Type directly from DCSA's system of record.
Asking the candidate to fill these in indicates either:
 (a) No DISS access — cannot actually verify, OR
 (b) Pre-offer action — §117.10(a)(7) violation
The phrase "this should suffice the clearance" confirms self-attested data is being
used as a substitute for an authoritative DISS JVS query.
Applicable rules: 32 CFR §117.10(a)(7), §117.10(h), SEAD 7
