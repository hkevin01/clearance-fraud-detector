# When to Provide Your SSN — and DCSA's Official Tools

> **All information on this page is sourced directly from dcsa.mil (verified April 10, 2026).**  
> Source pages: [dcsa.mil/mc/pv/mbi](https://www.dcsa.mil/mc/pv/mbi/), [dcsa.mil/is/systems](https://www.dcsa.mil/is/systems/), [dcsa.mil/Personnel-Vetting/Background-Investigations-for-Applicants](https://www.dcsa.mil/Personnel-Vetting/Background-Investigations-for-Applicants/)

---

## TL;DR — The One Rule

> **Your SSN goes into one place: the NBIS eApp portal at [eapp.nbis.mil](https://eapp.nbis.mil), after a conditional job offer, initiated by your FSO.**  
> Anyone asking for your SSN before that stage or by any other channel is not following the authorized process.

---

## Stage-by-Stage: When Your SSN Is (and Is Not) Required

| Stage | Who contacts you | SSN required? | Channel |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1. Resume / application** | Recruiter | ❌ NO | Job board / email |
| **2. Pre-screen / phone screen** | Recruiter | ❌ NO | Phone / video |
| **3. Technical interview** | Hiring team | ❌ NO | In-person / video |
| **4. Conditional offer** | HR / recruiter | ❌ NO (yet) | Offer letter |
| **5. FSO onboarding kick-off** | Facility Security Officer | ⚠️ Verbally to FSO only — directs you to eApp | Corporate phone/email |
| **6. SF-86 / background investigation form** | You, via eApp portal | ✅ YES — entered in eApp | [eapp.nbis.mil](https://eapp.nbis.mil) (HTTPS, CAC or username) |
| **7. Fingerprints** | FSO arranges; you appear in person | SSN already on file | In-person via SWFT |
| **8. Investigator interview** | DCSA Special Agent or CACI/Peraton contractor | SSN already on file | In-person or phone |

---

## Red Flags — Never Provide SSN When

- A **recruiter** asks for SSN at any point before Step 5
- Anyone asks you to email or text your SSN
- Someone says they need your SSN **"to verify your clearance in DISS"** — FSOs pull DISS records **from their own credentialed accounts** using your name and employer; they do not ask the candidate to supply SSN for this purpose
- Someone claims to be **DCSA** and cold-calls asking for your SSN — DCSA investigators present physical credentials in person and are verifiable (see Verification section below)
- The request comes via Telegram, WhatsApp, Signal, or any chat app
- The requester cannot name the cleared facility or provide a verifiable corporate email

## DCSA Systems You Will Directly Use (as an Applicant)

These are the only official portals where you personally enter sensitive data.

### eApp — `eapp.nbis.mil`
**What it is:** NBIS electronic application. This is where you complete your SF-86 (or SF-85/SF-85P) security questionnaire online.  
**When you use it:** After conditional offer, after your FSO initiates the background investigation request in NBIS.  
**What data goes in:** Full legal name, SSN, DOB, addresses, employment history, foreign contacts, financial info — everything on the SF-86.  
**Source:** [dcsa.mil/is/systems — eApp](https://www.dcsa.mil/Systems-Applications/)  

> ⚠️ Your FSO will send you an invitation link to eApp. You do **not** go to eApp on your own; you are invited in by your sponsoring facility.

### NBIS Subject Portal — `sps.nbis.mil`
**What it is:** Lets you check the status of your own background investigation and access prior completed investigations.  
**When you use it:** After your investigation is in progress.  
**Source:** [dcsa.mil/Personnel-Vetting/Background-Investigations-for-Applicants/Check-Your-Status](https://www.dcsa.mil/Personnel-Vetting/Background-Investigations-for-Applicants/Check-Your-Status/)

## DCSA Systems Your FSO Uses (You Will Not Log Into These)

These systems are accessed only by credentialed security officers and adjudicators — **not job candidates.** Understanding them helps you recognize fraud.

### DISS — Defense Information System for Security
**URL:** `dissportal.nbis.mil` (JVS) and `disscats.nbis.mil` (CATS/Appeals)  
**What it is:** Enterprise-wide system of record for personnel security, suitability, and credentialing for DoD military, civilian, and contractors. **Replaced JPAS on March 31, 2021.**  
**What FSOs use it for:** Documenting security clearance access, verifying eligibility determinations, tracking personnel security actions.  
**Sub-systems:**
- **DISS JVS (Joint Verification System)** — DoD professionals verify clearance eligibility
- **DISS CATS (Case Adjudication Tracking System)** — electronic adjudication records for clearances, HSPD-12, fitness
- **DISS Appeals** — supports DOHA and PSAB for subjects appealing adjudicative determinations  

**Fraud relevance:** When someone tells you "I need your SSN to pull you in DISS" — this is a fraud indicator. FSOs have their own credentialed DISS logins; any SSN stored in DISS is already on file from a prior investigation or formal application. An FSO has no legitimate reason to cold-collect your SSN verbally or by email as a "lookup trigger." (Source: NISPOM 32 CFR §117.10; dcsa.mil/Systems-Applications/Defense-Information-System-for-Security-DISS)

### CVS — Central Verification System
**What it is:** Used by security officers to determine if an existing adjudication or investigation already meets the current need before requesting a new one.  
**Fraud relevance:** FSOs check CVS before requesting a new investigation — a "clearance verification" request from a recruiter (not an FSO) is not how this works.

### SWFT — Secure Web Fingerprint Transmission — `swft.nbis.mil`
**What it is:** DoD system for cleared defense industry users to submit electronic fingerprints to DCSA.  
**When used:** Your FSO submits your fingerprints through SWFT; you appear in person for the fingerprint capture.  
**Source:** [dcsa.mil/Systems-Applications/Secure-Web-Fingerprint-Transmission-SWFT](https://www.dcsa.mil/Systems-Applications/Secure-Web-Fingerprint-Transmission-SWFT/)

### NBIS — National Background Investigation Services
**What it is:** The federal government's one-stop IT system for end-to-end personnel vetting. **eApp is the applicant-facing front end of NBIS.**  
**Source:** [dcsa.mil/Systems-Applications/National-Background-Investigation-Services-NBIS](https://www.dcsa.mil/Systems-Applications/National-Background-Investigation-Services-NBIS/)

### NISS — National Industrial Security System
**What it is:** System of record for industrial security oversight (facility clearances, contracts).  
**Relevant for:** Your employer's Facility Clearance (FCL) status — not individual clearances.

## Who Can Legitimately Contact You (and How)

From [dcsa.mil/Personnel-Vetting/Background-Investigations-for-Applicants/Verify-Your-Investigator](https://www.dcsa.mil/Personnel-Vetting/Background-Investigations-for-Applicants/Verify-Your-Investigator/):

> "DCSA Special Agents and contract Investigators carry credentials identifying them as representatives of DCSA. They will present their credentials when they introduce themselves to you."

- **DCSA investigators** may contact by phone, email, or text to schedule interviews — **they show credentials in person**
- **DCSA's current investigation contractors (as of Apr 2026):** CACI and Peraton
- These contractors must identify themselves **with the contractor name** when contacting you

### How to Verify a DCSA Investigator

|---|---|

## Background Investigation Forms Reference

| Form | Used for |
|---|---|
| **SF-86** | National Security positions (clearances — most common for contractors) |
| **SF-85** | Non-sensitive/low-risk public trust positions |
| **SF-85P** | Moderate-risk public trust positions |
| **SF-85PS** | Supplemental to SF-85P |

All forms are completed in **eApp** at [eapp.nbis.mil](https://eapp.nbis.mil) after FSO initiation.

## Summary Decision Tree

```
Recruiter asks for your SSN?
├── YES, before conditional offer → RED FLAG. Decline. Do not proceed.
└── NO / after offer

FSO asks for your SSN via:
├── Telegram / WhatsApp / Signal / text / personal email → RED FLAG
├── "To verify your clearance in DISS" (unsolicited) → RED FLAG
└── Corporate email directing you to eApp → NORMAL PROCESS

You're in eApp (eapp.nbis.mil), FSO-initiated, HTTPS, post-offer?
└── Fill out SF-86 completely including SSN → CORRECT AND REQUIRED
```
